About Me

Dylan Cole is a C# SDET, currently working as a consultant from Sparta Global. He has attention to detail, teamed with a strong work ethic and a willingness to learn new things. Perhaps in the future, he could be an author - as a side gig.

Formerly, Dylan worked seasonally as a Ride Operator at Stourport Fairground. Due to his personality, he was well-liked by both staff and clientelle. The fair is still owned by Henry Danter, and is currently run by his former partner Christine Collins. The ride was a Pinfari Dragon Coaster, and since the Italian rollercoaster manufacturing company has gone bankrupt, such a ride is incredibly rare!

Dylan was also an ambassador for Swansea University while doing his degree, and also did a small stint at an on-campus COVID-19 testing facility before Christmas of 2020. He also worked at TESCO during the first March 2020 lockdown, finding that it took around 4 weeks for new toilet rolls to be in stock!
